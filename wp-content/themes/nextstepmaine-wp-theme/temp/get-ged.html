• 1)Get a GED

    If your next step involves graduating from high school or taking the GED test (Test for General Educational Development), you’re in luck. There are 84 testing sites across the state, offered through Adult and Community Education programs. These programs offer orientation classes and pre-testing as well. View Information about GED & high school diploma programs through Maine Adult Education, along with the list of locations that offer GED testing programs in Maine.
