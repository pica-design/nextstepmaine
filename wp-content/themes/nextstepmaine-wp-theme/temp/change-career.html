• 1)Change My Career

    The majority of us have multiple careers during our lifetimes.  If you’re considering making a change, we’ve compiled some resources to help. Maine’s Career Centers are a great place to start assessing what your next step might be. They can provide information about how to decide what career is right for you, and how to get the right training to be qualified. To see the full list of career center locations Click here Jobs in demand in Maine provides a list of job fields around Maine that are desired by employers. The Maine Job Bank also has a list of jobs around the state of Maine that are desired by employers.
